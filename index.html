Introduction

This website will contain the information to setup a community BattleBit server.

Before you start, keep in mind:

  1. You will need to agree to and abide by a EULA in order to run your server.

  2. Your server will not have progression from the official stats. You will have to implement this. Examples include:
    • Players enter your server with official stats, however will not progress.
    • You connect your own backend database. This can include writing to disk, external databases, memory, etc.
